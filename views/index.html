E-SVADHYAYA.com


'Svadhyaya', as the term in itself means itself , ' Study by your own self'. E-Svadhyaya, is an all in one learning platform, which according to it's name, enables students to study by their self and own their own. Being independent students would be able to make their future strong by learning the topic of their choice from any teacher within the world. At the same time, E-Svadhyaya also offers the teachers and institutes to teach and impart thier knowledge worlwide online. It enable institutes to easily take their education online through many of it's advance features provided. Some other features are also there to become even financially independent and to make personality and future even more stronger i.e. Freelancing, job opportunities , selling recorded course video lectures , organising or taking webinars and many other advanced features also.
